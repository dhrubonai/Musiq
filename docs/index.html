— Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

Musiq comes packed with features that make listening to music a joy — not a chore.

Ad-Free Streaming

Listen without a single ad. No interruptions, no pop-ups, no distractions. Just music, pure and simple.

Offline Downloads

Save songs for when you have no internet. Perfect for travel, commutes, or areas with poor connectivity.

Synced Lyrics

Sing along with real-time, synchronized lyrics from multiple sources for maximum coverage.

Background Playback

Keep listening when the app is closed. Musiq plays while you use other apps or lock your phone.

Privacy First

No data collection. No tracking. No analytics. Your listening habits stay on your phone, period.

Sleep Timer

Fall asleep to music. Set a timer and Musiq gently fades out and stops when the time is up.

No Account Needed

Just install and listen. No sign-up, no email, no phone number. Optional Google sign-in for sync.

Premium Design

A clean, high-contrast interface that's easy on the eyes. Pure black for OLED screens saves battery.

Auto-Update

Musiq checks for new versions automatically. Get notified when an update is available — no manual checking.
