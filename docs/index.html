Everything you need.

Musiq comes packed with features that make listening to music a joy — not a chore.

Ad-Free Streaming

Listen to your favorite music without a single ad. No interruptions, no pop-ups, no distractions. Just music.

Offline Downloads

Save songs for when you have no internet. Perfect for travel, commutes, or areas with poor connectivity.

Synced Lyrics

Sing along with real-time, synchronized lyrics. Supports multiple lyrics sources for maximum coverage.

Background Playback

Keep listening when the app is closed. Musiq plays in the background while you use other apps or lock your phone.

Privacy First

No data collection. No tracking. No analytics. Your listening habits stay on your phone, period.

Sleep Timer

Fall asleep to music. Set a timer and Musiq will gently fade out and stop playing when the time is up.

No Account Required

Just install and start listening. No sign-up, no email, no phone number. Optional Google sign-in for playlist sync.

Premium B&W Theme

A clean, high-contrast black & white interface that's easy on the eyes. Pure black for OLED screens saves battery.

Auto-Update

Musiq checks for new versions automatically. When an update is available, you'll get a notification — no manual checking needed.
