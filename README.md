Musiq – Free Music Player

Musiq is a fast, modern, and completely free music player built for people who just want to enjoy music without unnecessary restrictions. There are no ads, no mandatory sign-up, and no subscription required. Install the app and start listening instantly.

Designed with a clean, elegant dark interface, Musiq offers a smooth and distraction-free experience whether you're streaming your favorite songs or listening offline. Powerful playback controls, synchronized lyrics, playlist management, and offline downloads make it easy to enjoy your music anytime.

For users who want a more personalized experience, Musiq also supports optional Google sign-in. After signing in, you can sync your playlists and music library with your YouTube Music account, giving you access to your saved music directly from Musiq.
